Basic porridge

Description

Porridge is one of our favourite healthy breakfast recipes. Quick and easy to prepare, whether in the microwave or on the stovetop, it's the perfect dish to enjoy as is, or to jazz up with your favourite toppings like fruit, spices and yoghurts - or just classic honey. With oats the primary ingredient in porridge, it's also one of the best breakfasts to have to maintain a healthy lifestyle. As they are low GI and full of dieatary fibre, they'll keep you fuller for longer.

Ingredients

  • 60g traditional rolled oats
  • 160ml skim milk

Steps

  1. Combine oats, milk and 2/3 cup (160ml) water in a small saucepan over medium heat. Bring to the boil. Cook, stirring, for 2-3 minutes or until oats are soft and creamy. Serve.
